# ADVENTURE SUMMARY: "MAZFROTH'S MIGHTY DIGRESSIONS"

## Adventure Overview
**Level Range:** Level 6 throughout (Cleric 1/Wizard 5) — campaign leveling changed to 1 level per adventure
**Location:** Candlekeep → Coast Way → Baldur's Gate (The Wide, Blackgate district)
**Duration:** Approximately 1 day in Candlekeep + 5 days travel + 1 day in Baldur's Gate

## Initial Hook
Auriel was reading *Mazfroth's Mighty Digressions* in one of Candlekeep's public reading halls when the book transformed into a gingwatzim — an ectoplasmic shapeshifter — and attacked him. He destroyed it with Mind Sliver and two 3rd-level Magic Missiles.

The Avowed (Reader Yolanda) arrived to investigate. First Reader Bookwyrm revealed this was the **third such incident** — three books donated as entry gifts had turned out to be monstrous creatures. He asked Auriel to investigate the source.

**Reward offered:** Helm of Comprehending Languages, upon finding who was responsible.

## Investigation in Candlekeep

### Witnesses Interviewed
Two seekers who had donated monstrous books were still in Candlekeep:

**Yalerion Highscroll** (middle-aged human book collector):
- Bought *Mazfroth's Mighty Digressions* at **Amberdune Books** in The Wide, Baldur's Gate
- Described a charming young male vendor and a silent woman working behind him
- Completely innocent — distressed to learn what he'd donated

**Valor** (tiefling bounty hunter):
- Bought *The Dark Hunger* at the same stall
- Sharp, straight-talking, had already suspected something was wrong
- Staying at Candlekeep voluntarily to make amends by helping with chores
- Also pointed to **Amberdune Books, The Wide, Baldur's Gate**

A third book (*Fallen Tethyamar*) had been donated by an unnamed seeker who had already left.

**Avowed provided:** 30 gp travel expenses

## The Road — Coast Way

### Night Four: Wererat Ambush
Mushika, a wererat, ambushed Auriel's camp with three giant rats.

**Auriel's tactics:**
- Sent Golden Dawn ahead to scout (Perception 20) — spotted ambush before it triggered
- Cast Slow (DC 14) on all four — all failed their saves
- Dispatched two rats with Magic Missile (2nd level) before they could act
- Killed remaining rat and drove off Mushika with additional Magic Missiles and Fire Bolt
- Pursued Mushika after he broke free of Slow — killed him with second Fire Bolt

**Combat summary:**
- Mushika: killed
- All three giant rats: killed
- Auriel: never took damage
- Resources spent: 3× 2nd level slots (Magic Missile), 1× 3rd level slot (Slow), 2 Fire Bolt cantrips

**Loot from Mushika:** 6 gp, 14 sp, silver ring (10 gp value)

## Baldur's Gate — The Wide

### Amberdune Books Stall
Auriel arrived fully rested. Before approaching, he ritually cast Detect Magic.

Within 30 feet of the stall, several books glowed with **transmutation auras** — gingwatzims in disguise. The stall was staffed by two jackalweres in humanoid form: a charming young man (front) and a cautious woman (rear).

**Auriel's approach:**
- Posed as a Candlekeep-bound seeker looking for an impressive donation book
- Observed the young man consistently steering him toward magical (fake) books
- Noticed the woman watching his hands specifically — looking for trouble

**The confrontation:**
Auriel ripped a page from one of the magical books, forcing it to reveal itself. The gingwatzim transformed in his hands. He immediately cast Slow on all three targets.

### Combat at the Stall
**Initiative:** Auriel (19) > Jackalwere woman (17) > Gingwatzim (8) > Young man (6)

**Round 1:**
- Auriel cast Tasha's Mind Whip on jackalwere woman (12 psychic damage, restricted to one action type next turn)
- Jackalwere woman moved to Auriel (used her one option on movement — Mind Whip restriction)
- Gingwatzim (adjacent from start, slowed) used Energy Drain — missed (AC 19)
- Young man tried to flee — triggered War Caster opportunity attack: Tasha's Mind Whip (8 psychic, restricted next turn). He still moved 15 ft away (slowed)

**Round 2:**
- Auriel: Magic Missile 2nd level — 2 darts on jackalwere woman (9 force, killed), 2 darts on young man (5 force)
- Young man: freed from Slow but Mind Whip restricted — used action for Sleep Gaze. Auriel saved (Wisdom 15 vs DC 10). Fey Ancestry = immune to magical sleep anyway
- Gingwatzim: still slowed, Energy Drain missed

**Round 3:**
- Auriel: Magic Missile 3rd level — 2 darts on young man (8 force, killed), 3 darts on gingwatzim (11 force)
- Gingwatzim took 15 necrotic from Energy Drain (Auriel used Shield reaction — wait, Shield was not used here; hit connected): Auriel took 15 necrotic damage. CON save 14 vs DC 12 — no exhaustion
- Gingwatzim: 28 HP remaining

**Round 4:**
- Auriel: Magic Missile 3rd level — 5 darts on gingwatzim (19 force). Gingwatzim at 9 HP
- Gingwatzim Energy Drain: missed

**Round 5:**
- Auriel: Toll the Dead (gingwatzim failed Wisdom save) — 12 necrotic (d12, target was missing HP). Gingwatzim dead.

**Combat total resources spent:**
- 3× 3rd level slots (Magic Missile ×3)
- 1× 2nd level slot (Magic Missile)
- 2× Tasha's Mind Whip (2nd level) — wait, these were 2nd level slots
- Corrected: 2× 2nd level slots (Tasha's Mind Whip), 1× 2nd level slot (Magic Missile) = 3× 2nd level total
- 3× 3rd level slots (Magic Missile ×3)
- HP taken: 15 necrotic (28/43 after combat)

**Watch arrived** during combat — Auriel explained the situation (Persuasion 21). Senior veteran accepted his account, secured the stall and remaining fake books.

## Blackgate District — Amberdune Hideout

### Preparation
Auriel took a long rest before approaching at night — full resources restored.

Ritually cast Unseen Servant and Detect Magic before entry.

### Entry
Front door unlocked. Auriel slipped in quietly (Stealth 12 — barely adequate).

Moved through the hideout. Detected no magical auras except on items noted.

### Confrontation with Korvala
Auriel located the leader — a woman in one of the upper rooms — and slipped in alone, closing the door behind him.

**Key dialogue:**

Auriel: *"Don't say a word. I just want to talk."*

Korvala explained:
- Their matriarch **Nidalia** was killed by adventurers who assumed she was a monster
- Resurrection requires a 1,000 gp diamond plus a capable spellcaster
- The gingwatzim scheme was to raise money — buyers paid for rare books and got convincing fakes
- The gingwatzims were never meant to attack anyone; they broke instructions when starving
- She never intended books to end up in Candlekeep

**Insight check: 24** — Completely genuine. Real grief, real purpose.

**Auriel's response:**
- Refused to allow the scheme to continue
- Suggested selling their legitimate book stock honestly instead
- Took the original books (the real *Mazfroth's Mighty Digressions*, *The Dark Hunger*, *Fallen Tethyamar*) — they belong to Candlekeep
- Suggested the pack could find honest work in libraries given their literary knowledge
- Noted that Nidalia would not have approved of distributing monsters

**Korvala's response:**
- Acknowledged Nidalia would have been horrified
- Agreed to stand down
- Gave Auriel the three original books from the basement (A6)
- Asked Auriel's name — exchanged names
- Offered future cooperation if Auriel returns to Baldur's Gate

**Pack casualties this adventure:**
- Fen and Salla (the two stall jackalweres) killed by Auriel at The Wide
- Remaining pack untouched
