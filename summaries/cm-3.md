# ADVENTURE SUMMARY: "THE JOY OF EXTRADIMENSIONAL SPACES"

## Adventure Overview
**Level Range:** Started at Level 4 (Cleric 1/Wizard 3), ended at Level 6 (Cleric 1/Wizard 5)
**Location:** Candlekeep and Fistandia's Extradimensional Mansion
**Duration:** Approximately 1 day in mansion + 3 days recovery/research

## Initial Hook
Auriel traveled to Candlekeep seeking Sage Matreous, an expert in curse removal. A town (possibly Auriel's home) has been afflicted with a terrible curse:
- Crops have failed completely this year
- Livestock stand withering like stick figures in fields
- Rain has dwindled to almost nothing
- A local mage identified it as a curse on the land itself

Matreous was known to be studying at Candlekeep in the fortress-library.

## The Discovery
Upon arrival at Study Room 7 (Matreous's assigned quarters), Auriel found:
- The room unoccupied but recently used
- *The Joy of Extradimensional Spaces* open on the desk
- The book: a treatise on extradimensional spaces (like *Mordenkainen's Magnificent Mansion*)
- Marginal notes by the mage Fistandia detailing how she created a permanent mansion
- The command word "scepter" written in arcane shorthand

Speaking "scepter" opened a shimmering portal in mid-air. Matreous appeared on the other side, trapped and unable to reopen the portal. He asked Auriel to explore the mansion and find the way to reopen it from within. Auriel agreed (Matreous owed him help with the curse in return).

When Matreous tried to leave through the portal carrying an imp figurine, the portal slammed shut and Matreous shrieked - the figurine's enchantment broke, releasing a real imp that attacked him.

## The Mansion - Overview
A permanent extradimensional mansion created by Fistandia, a powerful mage and priest of Mystra. The mansion exists outside normal space, surrounded by a swirling indigo miasma (causes exhaustion if entered). Three levels: Middle (main), Upper, and Lower.

**The Puzzle:** Seven books with gilded letters on their spines, scattered throughout the mansion. When arranged correctly, they spell the command word to reopen the portal: **LIBERTY**

## Middle Level Exploration (M1-M9)

### M1 - Foyer
Entry point from portal. Long hallways stretching left and right.

### M2 - Patio
Semicircular patio with gray flagstones, overlooking the miasma.

### M3 - Library
**Encounter:** Swarm of Animated Books
- Books attacked by knocking over a bookshelf (Auriel dodged with DEX save)
- Defeated with Fire Bolt (8 damage) and Toll the Dead (10 damage)
- **Loot:** Jeweled letter opener (20 gp), Puzzle book with letter **R**
- Books on arcana, natural science, religion, astrology, planar travel, poetry, mythology

### M4 - Exercise Room
- Training mannequin, weapon rack, martial diagrams
- Animated Broom cleaning the floor (left alone after failed attempt to pet it)
- **Loot:** Bandolier with 20 darts

### M5 - Study
- Fistandia's personal study with floor-to-ceiling bookshelves
- Fluffy black cat (friendly after successful Animal Handling)
- **Discovery:** Books revealing Fistandia was a mage/priest of Mystra who was granted this mansion by the goddess. She hid the command word across seven lettered books.
- Three paintings: green dragon, pegasus in flight, unicorn
- Secret door behind bookshelf (pull specific book to open) leading to stairs down to Lower Level
- **Loot:** Puzzle book with letter **I**

### M6 - Kitchen
**NPCs:** Cumin and Coriander (homunculi servants)
- Cumin: Created by Fistandia, cinnamon-colored
- Coriander: Created by Freyot (Fistandia's colleague), greenish
- Very helpful, eager to serve, haven't seen masters in years
- **Information provided:**
  - Masters (Fistandia and Freyot) left long ago but are alive (homunculi would die if masters died)
  - Warned about mimic that escaped from Lower Level (might be in dining room)
  - Described locations of puzzle books
  - Upper level dangers: flying swords (trophy room), animated bookshelf (chained library)
  - Fistandia used to disappear into planetarium for long periods
- Cats fed here in painted bowls

### M7 - Pantry
Stocked with dry goods, flour, vegetables, preserved meats. Nothing special.

### M8 - Dining Room
**Encounter:** Mimic (weakened)

- Disguised as seventh chair in corner (six chairs around table, one alone)
- Auriel cast Magic Missile to test it (10 damage)
- Mimic attacked, Auriel used Misty Step to escape and kited it with ranged spells
- Defeated with two Magic Missiles (10, 12 damage) and Toll the Dead (9 damage)
- **Note:** Mimic was gravely injured when captured, had only 30 HP total
- **Loot:** Silverware service for six (20 gp)
- Tapestries showing grapevines and winemaking

### M9 - Arboretum
- Beautiful garden with flowering shrubs and small trees
- Two glowing orbs providing light (driftglobes)
- Paved paths, semicircular patio
- Auriel deactivated driftglobes with "douse" command word
- **Loot:** 2 Driftglobes

## Lower Level Exploration (M17-M19)
Accessed via secret passage from M5 (Study)

### M17 - Alchemical Laboratory

- Long tables with vials, beakers, flasks of chemicals
- Scorch marks and acid burns from failed experiments
- Four clay figures (progression of homunculus creation - crude to lifelike)
- Research focused on transmuting materials into gold
- **Loot:** 50 gp worth of alchemical reagents, 2 Potions of Healing, Puzzle book with letter **B**

### M18 - Summoning Room
**Encounter:** Quasit (in toad form)
- Five-foot summoning circle with intricate runes
- Empty bookstand, three bronze braziers with cold cinders
- Smell of charcoal and sulfur
- Auriel spoke to the "toad" - it transformed and attacked
- Defeated with Magic Missile (9 damage) and Toll the Dead (11 damage)
- **Note:** This is where Fistandia summoned the imp that Matreous found

### M19 - Preserved Menagerie
- Glass vessels containing preserved creatures in fluid
- Collection: cockatrice, flumph, giant fire beetle, grell, myconid sprout, pseudodragon, four severed hands, slaad tadpole (still alive)
- One four-foot jar empty with lid missing - **the mimic escaped from here**
- Four severed hands = crawling claws (not encountered - jar unopened)
- Slaad tadpole left sealed (too dangerous to release)

## Upper Level Exploration (M10-M16)

### M10 - Landing/Stairs
- Decorative suit of armor with longsword on stand
- Trapdoor to attic in ceiling
- **Attic:** Dusty, unused, light visible through floorboards above M13, alternate access point to chained library

### M11 - Laboratory
- Long tables with scientific apparatus, books, specimens
- Cabinets with skulls, bones, mounted animals, minerals, dried plants, preserved creatures
- Floating orrery (magical model of celestial bodies)
- **Star Map on wall:** Night sky with golden sunburst in center, five stars depicted as blazing silver suns (solution to planetarium puzzle)
- Books on natural sciences, astronomy, astrology, physiology
- **Loot:** Puzzle book with letter **L**

### M12 - Planetarium
- **Illusion:** Appears as grassy knoll under starry night sky
- Five telescopes on bronze plates around central crystal sphere
- **Puzzle Solution:** Point each telescope at one of the five bright stars from M11's star map
- When aligned, starlight focuses through crystal sphere and reveals secret door to M13
- Auriel solved puzzle by matching telescopes to nearest bright stars

### M13 - Chained Library
**Encounter:** Animated Chained Library
- Books bound in iron covers, chained to shelves
- Enchanted to guard Fistandia's most precious knowledge
- Auriel tried using Mage Hand to grab puzzle book - triggered attack
- **Combat:**
  - Bookshelf has 45 HP, AC 14, can make 2 attacks (1d8+2 damage + grapple DC 12)
  - Auriel used Misty Step to escape grapple, kited with ranged attacks
  - Cast Web to restrain it (it broke free with STR check = 13)
  - Defeated with Magic Missile (11 damage), Shield (deflected attack), and Toll the Dead (12 damage)
- **Loot:** +1 Flail (book entitled *Martial Attack Techniques* with chain attached), Puzzle book with letter **T**

### M14 - Trophy Room
- Scarlet armchairs, reading tables with poetry/heroic fiction books
- Illusory fire in fireplace (no heat)
- Trophy heads on walls: stag, wolf, peryton, hell hound, black dragon wyrmling
- **Encounter:** Two Flying Swords above mantel
- Auriel preemptively attacked with Magic Missile at 2nd level (4 missiles: 9 + 4 damage)
- **Combat:**
  - First sword destroyed with Toll the Dead (7 damage)
  - Second sword attack deflected with Shield spell
  - Second sword resisted first Toll the Dead, destroyed by second (10 damage)
- **Loot:** Puzzle book with letter **Y**

### M15 - Bedroom
- Canopied bed with scarlet curtains
- Chest of drawers with jug and washbasin
- Painting of gold dragon on mountaintop
- Fluffy black cat napping (friendly)
- **Loot:** Puzzle book with letter **E**
- Auriel took short rest here (recovered 2 first-level spell slots via Arcane Recovery, spent 1d6 hit die for 6 HP)

### M16 - Balcony
- Wrought iron balcony overlooking arboretum
- Indigo miasma presses close
- No items or encounters

## Solving the Puzzle
After collecting all seven puzzle books (R, I, B, Y, E, L, T), Auriel arranged them to spell:

**L-I-B-E-R-T-Y**

A pulse of magical energy rippled through the mansion. The portal in the foyer reopened with a shimmering, musical chime.

## Return to Candlekeep

### The Imp Attack
Upon stepping through the portal back to Study Room 7:

- Found Matreous's body on the floor, dead from imp's poisoned sting
- Imp (invisible) waiting in ambush
- **Combat:**
  - Imp attacked with advantage (invisible), hit for 5 piercing damage, Auriel saved vs poison (CON save = 11)
  - Auriel drank Potion of Healing (recovered 7 HP) and cast Toll the Dead (imp saved)
  - Imp attacked again, missed
  - Toll the Dead failed again (imp saved)
  - Final Toll the Dead killed imp (11 necrotic damage) - exploded in black smoke
- **Current HP after fight:** 17/28

### Examination of Matreous
Found on body:
- Gray robes
- Belt pouch with 15 gp
- **Notebook with curse removal research** (valuable)
- Pendant with symbol of Deneir (god of writing)
- Puncture wound on neck from imp

## Securing the Mansion

### Meeting with Keeper Feldmar
Auriel reported everything to Keeper Feldmar (senior Avowed). Made case for being granted access to the mansion as a base of operations:

- Proven ability to navigate safely
- Solved all puzzles
- Better used than left empty
- Is a capable wizard who could make good use of it

**Persuasion check: 19** - Feldmar agreed to bring request to First Reader and council

### Three Days of Recovery & Research
While awaiting decision:

**Physical Recovery:**
- Rested in guest quarters
- Cast Cure Wounds on self (recovered 6 HP to 23/28)
- Two long rests brought to full HP (28/28)
- All spell slots and hit dice recovered

**Curse Research:**
- Studied Matreous's notebook + Candlekeep library
- **Investigation check with advantage: 25**
- **Findings:** Town's symptoms indicate blight curse or land desecration
- Possible causes: violated sacred ground, fiendish corruption, fey punishment, necromantic contamination
- Compiled notes on three potential cleansing rituals
- Would need to identify curse source first

**Networking:**
- **Persuasion: 14**
- Shared knowledge and mansion adventure story
- Made positive impression, people know Auriel's name
- **New acquaintances:**
  - Scribe Taelara (moon elf, planar magic)
  - Sage Korvin (elderly human, ancient history)
  - Novice Peren (gnome, admires Auriel's courage)

**Research on Fistandia & Freyot:**
- **History check with expertise: 15**
- Fistandia: transmutation/conjuration specialist, Mystra's priest, innovative with permanent spells
- Disappeared 8-10 years ago after leaving Candlekeep "for important research"
- Donated entire library collection to Candlekeep upon departure
- Freyot: colleague and friend of Fistandia, also transmutation/conjuration
- Disappeared same time, likely collaborating on major project
- Both still alive (homunculi would die if masters died)
- No one has heard from them in a decade

### The Council's Decision
Met with First Reader Bookwyrm (ancient gold dragon in human form) and senior Avowed.

**Persuasion with advantage: 21**

**Granted custodial rights to Fistandia's mansion** with conditions:
1. May use as base of operations and research space
2. Must maintain mansion and contents
3. Fistandia's claim supersedes if she returns
4. Must share significant discoveries with Candlekeep archives
5. Subject to Candlekeep rules when on premises

Received:
- Key to Study Room 7
- Scroll with command words ("scepter" and "liberty")
- Access recorded in Candlekeep archives under Auriel's name

  - Kitchen with automatic food preparation
