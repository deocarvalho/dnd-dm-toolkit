# ADVENTURE SUMMARY: "THE BOOK OF THE RAVEN"

## Adventure Overview
**Adventure Number:** 3
**Level Range:** Started at Level 6 (Cleric 1 / Wizard 5), ended at Level 7
**Location:** Candlekeep → Wytchway (abandoned) → Chalet Brantifax → Shadowfell Necropolis
**Duration:** Approximately 1 day in Candlekeep (research) + 3 days travel + 2 days at Chalet Brantifax + 1 night in the Shadowfell

---

## Initial Hook

A raven delivered a thin black-oak-covered book — *The Book of the Raven* — to Auriel's window at Candlekeep. Detect Magic confirmed it was mundane. Inside was a firsthand account of traveling with Vistani, and tucked between its pages, a hand-drawn treasure map with a verse pointing toward a remote highland location. Research identified the destination as **Chalet Brantifax**, an abandoned noble hunting lodge. Auriel departed to investigate.

---

## Key Events

### Research in Candlekeep
Auriel spent time researching the map's landmarks and the chalet's history:
- Landmarks confirmed as real — Hand and Horn, Threetree Hill, Scorch of the Red Wyrm
- Chalet Brantifax identified in old land registry as a hunting lodge built for Baron Brantifax, deceased, uninhabited since
- Sage Korvin noted the deliberate nature of the book's delivery — something chose Auriel specifically, or was desperate enough to reach out to anyone

### The Road — Wytchway
Wytchway, the hamlet marked on the map as the trail's starting point, was completely abandoned — rotted fences, dilapidated farmsteads, wild pigs. Monster attacks drove residents out years ago. No magical auras detected. Nothing of note found.

### Arrival at Chalet Brantifax
Chalet stands atop a remote foggy hill. Dozens of ravens perched silently on the roofline, watching Auriel with unsettling attentiveness. Golden Dawn reconnoitered by air — one entrance (front door, unlocked), graveyard to the south with four graves, one with disturbed earth.

### Exploration — Ground Floor
- **Cloakroom (entry):** Shovel and rake on hooks. Round-topped door west.
- **Den:** Dusty sheets over furniture, hunting trophies, painted-over hearth. Grand staircase up. Door south to kitchen.
- **Kitchen:** Old cooking implements, mice. Nothing of note.
- **Parlor:** Gold-inlaid table, overstuffed chairs, gilded harp, glass cabinet. Used as gathering space by the wereravens.

### Exploration — Cellar
- **Storage Cellar:** Empty crates and barrels, old furniture.
- **Servants' Quarters:** Two beds, wardrobes, candlesticks. Nothing of value.
- **Haunted Well (C3):** A whispering voice from the depths — *"Brorn! Where are you, boy?"* Auriel lowered a Light-enchanted bucket — 60 feet down to a flooded cistern. Sent Golden Dawn (resummoned as fish via Find Familiar ritual) to search the bottom. Found a platinum oval locket on a gold chain — *"For Lorna, Forever"* — containing a tiny portrait of the baroness.

### Exploration — Second Floor
- **Guest Rooms:** Both dusty, undisturbed for years. Nothing of note.
- **Study (with rolltop desk):** Desk locked. Auriel smashed it open. Found calligrapher's supplies and the **Baroness's Journal**. Spent one hour reading it — learned the full history of the Brantifax family (see Discoveries).
- **Master Bedroom:** Four-poster bed bolted to floor. Secret door behind southwest bedpost — twisted counterclockwise to open.

### Exploration — Tower (Baron's Loft & Upper Level)
- **Baron's Loft:** Explosion damage, destroyed staircase, soot. Worthless alchemical debris.
- **Upper Level:** Accessed via Misty Step. Unlocked chest with scorched lid — contained **Potion of Mind Reading** and **6 trinkets**. Returned via Feather Fall.

### Exploration — Third Floor
- **Servants' Attic:** Two holes in roof. Ravens watching. Padlocked trunk — smashed open. Contained studded leather armor, leather helm, heavy crossbow, monogrammed case (B.B.) with 15 crimson-fletched crossbow bolts, two hunting traps.
- **Attic Nursery:** Child's bed, rocking chair, dolls, washbasin, cradle, playpen, fish mobile. Undisturbed for decades. Nothing of note.
- **Storage Attic:** Two gilt mirrors, four oil paintings wrapped in canvas (pastoral deer scene, mastiff with duck, knight on hippogriff, baron and baroness portrait).

### The Graveyard — Day Investigation
Four graves: Baron Brantifax, Brorn (his mastiff), Heluthe (younger daughter), Sylphene (elder daughter).

**Baron's grave:** Dug up via Unseen Servant ritual. Found skeletal remains in rotted fine clothing and a **gold signet ring** (stag silhouette). Auriel placed the locket in the coffin with the baron's remains — his spirit was laid to rest. The whispering from the well stopped permanently.

**Sylphene's grave:** Dug up via Unseen Servant ritual. A **scarecrow** and two **crawling claws** burst from the coffin. Combat ensued.

### Combat — Scarecrow & Crawling Claws
- **Setup:** Auriel opened Sylphene's coffin; scarecrow and two crawling claws erupted from within
- **Round 1:** Scarecrow used Terrifying Glare (Auriel saved, WIS 20 vs DC 11). Auriel cast Burning Hands (force damage via Awakened Spellbook, DC 14) — all three failed saves, 12 force damage. Both crawling claws dead (2 HP each). Scarecrow took doubled fire damage = 24 (vulnerable) — badly hurt.
- **Round 2:** Scarecrow attacked twice — both missed AC 19. Auriel cast Toll the Dead — scarecrow failed WIS save, took 15 necrotic (d12, missing HP). Dead.
- **Resources spent:** 1× 1st level slot (Burning Hands)
- **Loot:** Nothing — scarecrow was pure construct

### Wereravens Revealed — Night
Auriel settled in the master bedroom for the night. Wereravens used mimicry to frighten him — creaking floorboards, dragging sounds above. Golden Dawn investigated — nothing found. Later, glass breaking in the kitchen — Golden Dawn spotted a humanoid shape fleeing. Auriel gave chase and called out, identifying himself and mentioning the Book of the Raven. The wereravens revealed themselves.

### Meeting the Scarlet Sash
Four wereravens in the parlor, lantern light, dusty black robes with scarlet sashes:

- **Madrina Natterask** (55, stocky, matriarch) — calm, matter-of-fact, quietly authoritative
- **Taspar Hatchhill** (71, tall, wispy white hair, lazy left eye) — nervous, polite, timid around strangers
- **Rennick Groka** (22, rail-thin, morose) — mistrustful, wise beyond years, deeply loyal to the kindness
- **Vinique** (30, wry wit, cackle laugh) — direct, decisive, carrying something she won't put down

Auriel recounted his investigation. Madrina confirmed the Scarlet Sash's purpose: collecting evil objects and keeping them from falling into the wrong hands, monitoring the shadow crossing in Sylphene's grave.

Key information exchanged:
- The shadow crossing leads to a Shadowfell necropolis
- The crossing only functions at night from an open grave
- Filling the grave closes the crossing temporarily
- Wereravens had never cleared the necropolis — too dangerous
- A wight named Drovath Harrn inhabits the largest mausoleum, wears a ring of jumping, uses a longbow from rooftops
- Twelve ghouls hide in open graves
- Two gargoyles perch on the mausoleum roof

Auriel decided to cross alone. Prepared spells, completed long rest.

### The Shadowfell — Necropolis

Auriel descended into Sylphene's open grave. Shadow crossing transported him to the Shadowfell necropolis — circular, 300 feet across, permanent twilight, ash-grey sky.

#### Combat 1 — Two Gargoyles
- **Setup:** Two gargoyles on mausoleum roof 30 feet north. Auriel opened with Fire Bolt before initiative.
- Gargoyle 1 took 10 fire damage (pre-initiative Fire Bolt) + 13 fire damage (Fire Bolt round 1) + 13 force damage (Burning Hands, Awakened Spellbook) = dead
- Gargoyle 2 took 13 force damage (Burning Hands) + 15 necrotic (Toll the Dead) = dead
- Auriel took 6 + 5 = 11 piercing damage before using Shield on third hit
- **Resources spent:** 1× 1st level slot (Burning Hands with Awakened Spellbook), 1× 1st level slot (Shield)
- **HP after:** 23/34

#### Combat 2 — Drovath Harrn (Wight) + 12 Ghouls
- **Setup:** Auriel cast Fly (3rd level) and hovered 15 feet up to draw ghouls together while engaging wight at range
- Tasha's Mind Whip × 3 on wight (2nd level slots): 11 + 10 + 11 = 32 psychic damage total, restricted wight's actions each turn
- Wight fired longbow three times: first hit (9 damage) blocked by Shield; second missed; third missed
- All 12 ghouls clustered beneath Auriel, unable to reach him at 15 feet
- Burning Hands (force, 1st level) caught 8 ghouls: 5 failed (11 force each = dead), 3 succeeded (5 force each)
- Remaining ghouls finished with Fire Bolt cantrip (multiple rounds)
- Magic Missile (3rd level, 5 darts) on wight for 19 force damage = dead (had 13 HP remaining)
- **Resources spent:** 3× 2nd level (Tasha's Mind Whip ×3), 1× 3rd level (Fly), 1× 3rd level (Magic Missile), 1× 1st level (Burning Hands), 1× 1st level (Shield)
- **All spell slots expended after this combat except cantrips**

#### Harrn Mausoleum — Search
- Central chamber: horse bones of three warhorses, two closed sarcophagi (inanimate bones), Drovath's open sarcophagus (empty, lid broken)
- Passive Perception 16 exceeded DC 13 — noticed hidden compartment in base of Drovath's sarcophagus
- Inside: **Saddle of the Cavalier** (Drovath's, kept by Auriel)
- Taking the saddle animated three warhorse skeletons

#### Combat 3 — Three Warhorse Skeletons
- **Setup:** Auriel took saddle and moved toward exit; skeletons rose
- Misty Step (used 3rd level — last slot) to teleport outside gate, avoiding opportunity attacks
- Fire Bolt on nearest skeleton: 10 fire damage (11 HP remaining)
- Flew up and out of reach (10-foot reach from Large creature's space edge)
- Remaining skeletons finished with Fire Bolt cantrip over subsequent rounds
- **Resources spent:** 1× 3rd level (Misty Step — final slot)
- **All spell slots now fully expended**

### Return to Material Plane
Auriel lay in the shadow crossing to return. Wereravens had filled the grave — Auriel was buried six feet underground. Two failed Athletics checks (DC 13, STR -1) before succeeding on third. Emerged covered in grave dirt to Vinique's laughter.

### Departure from Chalet Brantifax
- Auriel donated the Book of the Raven and the Baroness's Journal to Candlekeep via Bookwyrm
- Returned the 6 trinkets to the wereravens (they declined, said Auriel had earned them)
- Accepted the **Orcus Figurine** (in nonfunctional lantern) from Vinique for delivery to Bookwyrm
- Loaded all salvage onto Floating Disk ritual and departed

### Return to Candlekeep
- Delivered full account to Bookwyrm
- Donated Book of the Raven and Baroness's Journal to the library
- Delivered Orcus Figurine lantern — Bookwyrm locked it in an iron-banded cabinet personally
- Bookwyrm arranged Avowed contacts to sell Auriel's salvage at better prices
- Bookwyrm granted access to restricted collection for spell research
- **Charm of Heroism** granted for laying Baron Brantifax's spirit to rest

## Puzzles & Discoveries

**Baron's spirit in the well:** Whispering voice said *"Brorn! Where are you, boy?"* — the baron's sleepwalking ghost, drowned in the well searching for his dead dog. Reuniting his locket (portrait of his wife, found at the bottom of the cistern) with his remains in the graveyard laid him to rest.

**Baroness's Journal:** Revealed full family history — baron died in the well while sleepwalking after his mastiff Brorn died; Sylphene (elder daughter) died at age 6, likely killed by the baroness who found her deformities shameful; Heluthe (younger daughter) killed by a wolf at age 9 while hunting; both daughters buried at baron's insistence; baroness fled citing "evil whispers" in the graveyard.

**Secret door in master bedroom:** Bedpost nearest southwest wall twisted counterclockwise to open.

**Hidden compartment in Drovath's sarcophagus:** Passive Perception 16 vs DC 13 — Saddle of the Cavalier found.

**Warhorse skeletons trigger:** Removing the saddle from the mausoleum animates three warhorse skeletons to attack.

**Shadow crossing:** Sylphene's open grave is a shadow crossing to the Shadowfell. Functions only at night, only from an open grave. Filling the grave temporarily closes it. Time moves differently — Auriel was gone ~2 hours in the Shadowfell; nearly no time passed in the material plane.
