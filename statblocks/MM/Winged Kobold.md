>## Winged Kobold
>*Small humanoid (kobold), Lawful Evil*
>___
>- **Armor Class** 13
>- **Hit Points** 7 (3d6 - 3)
>- **Speed** 30 ft., Fly 30 ft.
>- **Initiative** +3 (13)
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|7 (-2)|16 (+3)|9 (-1)|8 (-1)|7 (-2)|8 (-1)|
>___
>- **Senses** Darkvision 60 Ft., Passive Perception 8
>- **Languages** Common, Draconic
>- **Challenge** 1/4 (XP 50; PB +2)
>- **Proficiency Bonus** +2
>___
>***Sunlight Sensitivity.*** While in sunlight, the kobold has disadvantage on attack rolls, as well as on Wisdom ({@skill Perception}) checks that rely on sight.  
>
>***Pack Tactics.*** The kobold has advantage on an attack roll against a creature if at least one of the kobold's allies is within 5 feet of the creature and the ally isn't {@condition incapacitated}.  
>
>### Actions
>
>***Dagger.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 5} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}5 ({@damage 1d4 + 3}) piercing damage.  
>
>***Dropped Rock.*** {@atk rw} {@hit 5} to hit, one target directly below the kobold. {@h}6 ({@damage 1d6 + 3}) bludgeoning damage.
