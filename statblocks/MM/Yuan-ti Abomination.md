>## Yuan-ti Abomination
>*Large monstrosity (shapechanger, yuan-ti), Neutral Evil*
>___
>- **Armor Class** 15 (natural armor)
>- **Hit Points** 127 (15d10 + 45)
>- **Speed** 40 ft.
>- **Initiative** +3 (13)
>___
>|STR|DEX|CON|INT|WIS|CHA|
>|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
>|19 (+4)|16 (+3)|17 (+3)|17 (+3)|15 (+2)|18 (+4)|

>- **Skills** Perception +5, Stealth +6
>- **Immunities** Poison; Poisoned
>- **Senses** Darkvision 60 Ft., Passive Perception 15
>- **Languages** Abyssal, Common, Draconic
>- **Challenge** 7 (XP 2,900; PB +3)
>- **Proficiency Bonus** +3
>___
>***Shapechanger.*** The yuan-ti can use its action to polymorph into a Large snake, or back into its true form. Its statistics are the same in each form. Any equipment it is wearing or carrying isn't transformed. It doesn't change form if it dies.  
>
>***Magic Resistance.*** The yuan-ti has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.  
>
>***Innate Spellcasting (Abomination Form Only).*** The yuan-ti's innate spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save {@dc 15}). The yuan-ti can innately cast the following spells, requiring no material components:  
>
>At will: {@spell animal friendship} (snakes only)  
>1/day: {@spell fear}  
>3/day: {@spell suggestion}  
>
>### Actions
>
>***Multiattack (Abomination Form Only).*** The yuan-ti makes two ranged attacks or three melee attacks, but can use its bite and constrict attacks only once each.  
>
>***Bite.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature. {@h}7 ({@damage 1d6 + 4}) piercing damage plus 10 ({@damage 3d6}) poison damage.  
>
>***Constrict.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 10 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) bludgeoning damage, and the target is {@condition grappled} (escape {@dc 14}). Until this grapple ends, the target is {@condition restrained}, and the yuan-ti can't constrict another target.  
>
>***Scimitar (Abomination Form Only).*** {@atk mw} {@hit 7} to hit, reach 5 ft., one target. {@h}11 ({@damage 2d6 + 4}) slashing damage.  
>
>***Longbow (Abomination Form Only).*** {@atk rw} {@hit 6} to hit, range 150/600 ft., one target. {@h}12 ({@damage 2d8 + 3}) piercing damage plus 10 ({@damage 3d6}) poison damage.
