>- **Senses** Blindsight 60 Ft. (Blind Beyond This Radius), Passive Perception 6
>- **Languages** —

>***Antimagic Susceptibility.*** The rug is {@condition incapacitated} while in the area of an {@spell antimagic field}. If targeted by {@spell dispel magic}, the rug must succeed on a Constitution saving throw against the caster's spell save DC or fall {@condition unconscious} for 1 minute.  
>
>***Damage Transfer.*** While it is grappling a creature, the rug takes only half the damage dealt to it, and the creature {@condition grappled} by the rug takes the other half.  
>
>***False Appearance.*** While the rug remains motionless, it is indistinguishable from a normal rug.  
>
>### Actions
>
>***Smother.*** {@atk mw} {@hit 5} to hit, reach 5 ft., one Medium or smaller creature. {@h}The creature is {@condition grappled} (escape {@dc 13}). Until this grapple ends, the target is {@condition restrained}, {@condition blinded}, and at risk of suffocating, and the rug can't smother another target. In addition, at the start of each of the target's turns, the target takes 10 ({@damage 2d6 + 3}) bludgeoning damage.
